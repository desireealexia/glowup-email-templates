GSkincare Myth Busted: Double Cleansing

If you're still relying on one face wash to do it all, this one's for you. In Korean skincare, double cleansing is the golden first step - because clean skin is the foundation of everything that follows.

Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Oil Cleanser: melts away makeup, sunscreen & excess sebum
  • Step 2: Water-Based Cleanser: removes leftover sweat, dirt & impurities

Why bother? Because clean skin = better absorption for toners, serums, and moisturisers. It's gentle, effective, and totally skin-barrier-safe.
